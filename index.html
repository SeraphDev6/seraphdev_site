I have been exploring both FDM and SLA printing on my own, using models foound on the internet, as well as my own models made with Blender and TinkerCAD.

Computers have integrated themselves into our daily lives, and can provide immense value to those able to use them effectively. Education

Frank Oppenheimer once said The best way to learn is to teach. I wholeheartedly agree with this statement and have spent several years developing and implementing STEM curriculum for children ages 5-14.
